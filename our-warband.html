Our Warband - Da Brewmasters

Welcome to The Grinning Orc, run by me, Gromk Snagglefang, and my... uh... talented crew of brewmasters (mostly grots I keep an eye on). We've been mixin' up potent potables since... well, for a while now!

Our philosophy is simple: if it bubbles, it's probably good. If it explodes, it was probably *too* good (or one of da grots messed up again). We source our ingredients from the wildest, most dangerous places, 'cause that's where the best stuff grows. Or crawls.

Meet Da Team (The Ones That Haven't Run Off):

  • Gromk Snagglefang: Head Brewboss. Tastes everything. Survived most of it.
  • Zoggit the "Wise": Chief Grot Stirrer. Surprisingly good at not blowing things up... usually.
  • Snark Littletooth: Ingredient "Acquisitions" Specialist. Very good at finding things that aren't his.
  • Various other Grots: For... uh... moral support and heavy lifting.

So come on in, grab a brew, and try not to annoy da Warboss!
